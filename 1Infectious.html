#1: Tobu - Infectious

Description About the Song:

"Infectious" is a song by Tobu released on June 22, 2014. It was previously available on NCS' SoundCloud account until it was removed due to the expiration of Tobu's contract with the label. The song is now copyright-free and has gained significant popularity, with over 1,161,198 plays on SoundCloud as of March 28, 2016. The song's background shares similarities with "She Got Me Like," and it was trimmed from its original mix on YouTube.

Description About the Author:

Tobu, whose real name is Toms Burkovskis, is a Latvian music producer known for his uplifting and melodic house music. He began his music journey at the age of 11 and has since created a significant impact on the electronic music scene. His work is characterized by catchy melodies and energetic beats, often released through platforms like NoCopyrightSounds (NCS). Tobu's music is inspired by the works of electronic music producers like Deadmau5 and Avicii, and he has been a prolific artist on the NCS label. His most popular song, "Candyland," became a viral phenomenon on YouTube, solidifying his place in the online electronic music community. Tobu maintains a relatively private persona, focusing on his music and avoiding live performances.
